About Me

Hello, my name is Taehwan (Danny) Lee and I am a Graphic Designer and Illustrator from Rhode Island School of Design. I am proficient with the Adobe Master collection and 3D modeling programs such as Blender. I also have experience working with HTML and CSS. In college, I’ve done many projects working with Information Design, Logo Design, 3D modeling, animation, and Digital Painting.

When it comes to design, creating elegant and dynamic concepts is my specialty. Having a strong concept in mind helps the artist create a detailed roadmap of where he or she wants the design to go. Without a strong idea serving as the foundation, then the overall design will be unfocused and unbalanced. Before the design goes underway, I believe that mapping the layout of a whole design though a concept map is essential to create a flawless design.

I belive simplicity is an important factor when it comes to good design. It takes about a minute for the consumer to lose interest while trying to use a product. A straightforward and witty design is the best way to keep the consumers’ attention on the product. Making a complex design with convoluded procedures will only frustrate the user. Simply put, the best approach to achieving a good design is through a strong concept.

In my free time, I enjoy other forms of art and design such as sewing, concept art, figure drawing, crafting stage props, and music. I am fairly skilled in the bass guitar and piano.

"True simplicity is derived from so much more than just the absence of clutter and ornamentation. It's about bringing order to complexity."

Joney Ive, Apple Inc.
